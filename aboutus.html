About Palzo Media

We Love Food and People

One day, we noticed something simple: people love looking at delicious food online, and restaurants want more people to come try their food. That gave us a big idea! What if we could help restaurants show off their meals in a way that makes people excited to visit?

Pictures That Make You Hungry

We take fun, beautiful photos and videos of the food restaurants make — the kind that makes people say, “Let’s eat there!” Our job is to help restaurants look amazing online so more people come in, try the food, and tell their friends. It’s all about making real connections through great content..

Helping Restaurants Shine

We know running a restaurant is hard work, and not everyone has time to post on social media. That’s where we come in. We take care of the online stuff so restaurant owners can focus on cooking and caring for their guests. We’re proud to help bring more happy customers to the places that make our favorite meals.
